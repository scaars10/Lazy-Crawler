Traffic Awareness Human Chain
American Society of Civil Engineers - (ASCE) International Student Chapter of PEC University of Technology, organised a traffic awareness human chain, in which members of this society urged masses to follow traffic rules and regulations through attractive posters and slogans. The initiative was taken by the members of this society ahead of the festive season wherein people indulge into activities such as rash driving, drunken driving, honking near hospitals, traffic congestions etc. This event was attended by Faculty members and more than 80 Students of the PEC.
