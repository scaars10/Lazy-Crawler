Online Survey for IIMs Training for PEC Faculty
National Project Implementation Unit (NPIU)
The Professional Development Trainings (PDTs) to Head of the institutions and Senior Faculty is one of the important activity envisaged in the Project Implementation Plan of the Project.
Before commencement of such trainings, an online survey is being conducted by NPIU with objective to serve the purpose of identification of challenges being faced by administrators of technical universities/institutions/colleges. The output of this survey will be used to design curricula for Professional Development Training at IIMs for the participants so as to make it effective and need based.
Therefore, you are requested to go through the below mentioned link and give your responses before 22nd September 2017.
Click Here to fill the Online Survey
The participants viz. Principal, Director, TEQIP coordinator, TEQIP nodal officers, all professors, associate professors, registrar and admin officer can participate mandatorily in the survey.
Please treat this as MOST URGENT.
