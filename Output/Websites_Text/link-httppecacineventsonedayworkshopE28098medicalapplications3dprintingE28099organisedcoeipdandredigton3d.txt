One day workshop on ‘Medical Applications of 3D Printing’ organised by CoE (I&Pd) and Redigton 3D
A one day workshop on Medical Applications of 3D Printing was organised by Centre of Excellence in Industrial and Product Design on 20 th Aug, 2017 at Hyatt Regence, Chandigarh. The workshop attracted was attended by industry experts, doctors and students. The event was inaugurated by Dr. Parveen Kalra, Coordinator, Centre of Excellence in Industrial and Product Design. The workshop started with an in-depth analysis and review of the current industry trends. The participants were introduced and exposed to the far reaching applications of 3D printing technology in medicine. Representatives from CoE and Redington 3D also discussed some selected projects. Mr Santosh Nair, Senior General Manager, Redington solved many queries and doubts of the participants regarding the technology.
