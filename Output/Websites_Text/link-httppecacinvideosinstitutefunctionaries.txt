Inaugral Video for PECFEST 2015PECfest is the annual cultural and technical fest held on a massive scale. It is an extravaganza of talent and effort, and this year was held from 31st October 2014-3rd November 2014. The fest witnessed an average total footfall of approximately 25000 people, and has been graced with the performance of numerous national and international stars. More than 500 students were involved in organising the fest from first year to final year with all their zeal, dedication and hardwork to make the fest a grant success.
