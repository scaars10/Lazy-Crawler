Recent Research Activities
Nanoelectronic Device Fabrication and Characterisation ToolsExpert Lecture
Various nano- and micro-electronic device fabrication techniques and characterisation tools used for the fabrication of electronic devices and various programmes under Indian Nanoelectronics User Program (INUP) funded by Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Govt. of India were discussed.
Remote Sensing Based Maps and Geospatial InformationNational Workshop
Being a joint initiative by ISRO and ISRS, the workshop was an enlightening affair.The audience witnessed the congregation of some brilliant minds of the nation in an exceedingly revealing series of lectures and discussion through live streaming. Director SASE(DRDO) and eminent scientists from DRDO also graced the occasion with their presence.
Advanced Electronics and Photonic DevicesMOU
PEC University of Technology, Chandigarh and Semi-Conductor Laboratory (SCL), an autonomous body under Department of Space, Government of India have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for a period of five years for collaboration in the areas of Advanced Electronics and Photonic Devices including CMOS Circuits and MEMS Design.
