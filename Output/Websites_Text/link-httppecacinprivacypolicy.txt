The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for the PEC University of Technology Homepage (http://www.pec.ac.in).
Information Gathering
We along with the hosts of this site, use your IP address to administer our Website by identifying which parts of the site are mostly used. We also use the search terms that a user enters into the search engine to improve the efficiency of the engine and the site. We assure that this information shall remain anonymous.
The information gathered is to generate statistical reports, which at no time disclose site usage by individual IP addresses. Such information is retained temporarily and then deleted from the servers completely.
